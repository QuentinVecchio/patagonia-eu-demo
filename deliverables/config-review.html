3. Brand Voice

Personality

direct mission-driven utilitarian nature-connected activist

Tone

Confident and knowledgeable without being preachy. Speaks like a trusted friend who happens to be an environmental scientist and avid climber. States facts plainly. Lets the product's purpose and construction speak for itself rather than relying on superlatives.

Preferred Vocabulary (30 terms)

built for designed for made with recycled polyester recycled nylon Fair Trade Certified™ Worn Wear Ironclad Guarantee repair last a lifetime keep gear going longer PrimaLoft® Gold DWR Yulex® NetPlus® P.U.R.E.™ technology Capilene® H2No® lightweight packable weather-resistant windproof breathable bluesign® approved responsibly sourced take action grassroots protect restore

Avoid Words (31)

luxury premium exclusive trendy stylish fashionable must-have game-changer revolutionary cutting-edge best-in-class world-class next-level elevate curated eco-friendly green sustainable carbon-neutral guilt-free fast fashion limited edition influencer vibe slay obsessed

Sentence Style

Short declarative sentences. Lead with what the product does or who it serves. Headlines: 3–7 words. Body: 15–30 words. Paragraphs: 2–4 sentences max.

Example Phrases

  • "We built the Nano Puff® Jacket for climbers who needed a weather-resistant, lightweight and packable synthetic insulation layer."
  • "One of the most responsible things we can do for the planet is to keep our gear going longer."
  • "We guarantee everything we make."
  • "If it's broken, let's fix it."
